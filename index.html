About

I got into this work sideways.

I did SDGs field research for my master — working with immigrant students in Japanese schools on language, identity, and belonging. It taught me something I've carried into every commercial role since: the hardest problems aren't technical — it's about creating a space where people feel comfortable enough to show up as themselves — and then comes the translation: of culture, context, and trust.

Over five years in consulting, key account management, and e-commerce growth, I've learned that markets have personality, accounts have histories, and the best commercial decisions usually require someone who can hold both the data and the human context at the same time.

I'm Chinese, built my career across Japan and Asia-Pacific, and I'm now in Germany. The connective tissue across all of it is the same: I translate — across cultures, between data and decisions, between what a brand needs and what an account will actually move on. Looking for BD, account management, or commercial strategy roles where that cross-cultural reading is the edge.
