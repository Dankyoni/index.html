05 — Synthesis · Expansion Framework

Four Archetypes Across the Ecosystem

Archetype 01
Local Manufacturing
BYD · Leapmotor × Stellantis
Highest capital commitment. Bypasses EU tariffs, qualifies for local content incentives. Longest timeline to revenue.
Archetype 02 · Dominant
Equipment Export
EN+ · Autel · Sungrow · Gresgying · East · Iocharger · EVbee
Fastest-scaling model in 2024. CE-certified hardware + local service subsidiary. Asset-light but tariff-exposed.
Archetype 03
Strategic Partnerships
Star Charge · Autel · NIO
Deep integration with EU utilities, interoperability platforms, and auto groups. Highest strategic moat over time.
Archetype 04 · Emerging
SaaS / Platform Play
YKC · DAKEYUN
Software-only international model — aggregation platform or SaaS to EU CPOs without owning hardware. Nascent as of 2024.
Segmentation for account targeting: Archetype 03 — highest-priority strategic dialogue · Archetype 02 — distribution & supply chain · Archetype 01 — long-cycle anchor accounts.
