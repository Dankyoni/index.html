Layer 2 — Charging Point Operators (CPOs)

Three CPO Models — One Clear EU Leader

Hardware-Integrated CPO
Own Assets · EU-Deployed
Star Charge ★ Most Advanced
Operational in Germany, France, Belgium, Italy, Norway, UK. 2018 Hubject partnership · 2024 Schneider Electric collaboration (new-energy equipment + microgrid + inverter + lithium storage R&D)
TELD
CE cert obtained Jan 2023 (160KW DC); EN 61557-8 component cert May 2024. China's #2 CPO by terminal share. EU deployment early stage but further along than most peers.
State-Owned Grid
Domestic Scale · EU Nascent
State Grid (SGCC)
China's largest charging network by coverage. High EU entry barriers — regulatory and commercial. Exploratory positioning only as of 2024.
CSG (Southern Grid)
Southern China focus. Monitoring EU market as long-term strategic option; no confirmed EU commercial activity.
SaaS / Platform Model
Software-First · Emerging
YKC (云快充)
China's largest 3rd-party EV charging SaaS — ~17% domestic share via aggregation platform (not owned hardware). Investors: GLP · CATL · NIO Capital · OPPO · ABB. International expansion via GLP global network.
DAKEYUN
Only Chinese CPO attempting pure SaaS international model — customizable station management SaaS for Chinese charger makers going overseas (since 2023). EU market still nascent.
Software-only EU entry is still nascent — only DAKEYUN has attempted it as of 2024. Most CPOs must build hardware presence before software can follow. Star Charge is the only Chinese CPO with confirmed multi-country EU operational assets.

EU DC fast-charging is still nascent — European public networks remain predominantly slow AC, while China is DC-majority. Chinese exporters proven at DC scale face a large structural growth runway in Europe.
Common pattern across all 8 players: CE certification → local subsidiary build-out → utility/grid partnerships. Only EVbee has committed to EU local manufacturing — most stay asset-light, exporting from China.
Synthesis — Expansion Framework

Four Archetypes Across the Ecosystem

Archetype 01
Local Manufacturing
BYD · Leapmotor × Stellantis
Highest capital commitment. Bypasses EU tariffs, qualifies for local content incentives. Longest timeline; strongest long-term strategic moat.
Archetype 02 · Dominant
Equipment Export
EN+ · Autel · Sungrow · Gresgying · East · Iocharger · EVbee
Fastest-scaling model in 2024. China-manufactured hardware with CE cert + local service subsidiary. Asset-light but tariff-exposed.
Archetype 03
Strategic Partnerships
Star Charge (Hubject, Schneider) · Autel (CPO network) · NIO (BaaS)
Deep integration with European utilities, interoperability platforms, and automotive groups. Builds highest strategic moat over time.
Archetype 04 · Emerging
SaaS / Platform Play
YKC · DAKEYUN
Software-only international model — aggregation platform or SaaS to European CPOs without owning hardware. Still nascent in Europe as of 2024.
Segmentation for account targeting: Archetype 03 — highest-priority strategic dialogue · Archetype 02 — distribution & supply chain partnerships · Archetype 01 — long-cycle anchor accounts.
